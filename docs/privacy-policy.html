Privacy Policy — Orthodox Calendar

Last updated: 14 June 2026

Orthodox Calendar ("the app") is developed by Bojan Mihovilović ("we", "us"). We respect your privacy. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. The short version: we do not collect, store, or share any personal data, and there are no accounts, ads, or tracking.

Information we collect

None. The app has no user accounts and does not require any sign-in. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information about you, and we do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.

Device calendar access

The app requests permission to read and write your device's calendar (READ_CALENDAR / WRITE_CALENDAR). This is used solely so that you can add a feast or commemoration to your own calendar as a reminder, and only when you explicitly choose to do so. Calendar events are created on your device through the system calendar. We never read, collect, upload, or transmit your calendar data, and the app does not access your calendar in the background.

Internet access

The app uses the internet (INTERNET permission) only to download calendar data for years that are not already bundled in the app. These requests fetch read-only liturgical calendar files from our hosting service. No personal information is sent with these requests, and the data is cached on your device so it remains available offline afterward. The current year's data is bundled in the app and works with no internet connection at all.

Data stored on your device

Your app settings (such as chosen language and theme) and any calendar data downloaded for offline use are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device, and it is removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

Third parties

We do not share any information with third parties, because we do not collect any. The app contains no third-party advertising or analytics software development kits.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
