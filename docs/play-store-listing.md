**App title** (17)
```
Orthodox Calendar
```

**Short description** (78)
```
Daily Orthodox saints, scripture, fasting & saints' lives — fully offline.
```

**Full description**
```
Orthodox Calendar is a clean, fully offline companion for the Orthodox Christian liturgical year. Open it any day to see the saints commemorated, the appointed scripture readings, the fasting guidance for the day, and in-depth biographies of the saints.

Built for daily use — fast, ad-free, and private. Nothing to sign in to, and the current year works with no internet connection at all.

FEATURES

• Daily commemorations — saints and feasts for every day of the year
• Scripture readings — the epistle and gospel appointed for the day
• Fasting guidance — clear daily fasting rules with a color legend
• Saints' lives — full biographies for major saints and feasts
• List and grid views — browse a month at a glance or day by day
• Jump to any date with the date picker, or tap Today to return
• Search saints by name
• Add feasts and commemorations to your device calendar as reminders
• Share the day's commemoration with friends and family

CALENDARS & LANGUAGES

• Serbian (Old / Julian Calendar)
• Russian (Old Calendar)
• English — Old Calendar and New (Revised Julian) Calendar

Choose the tradition and language that fit your parish. Light and dark themes follow your system or can be set manually.

PRIVACY

No account, no ads, no tracking. The app reads and writes your device calendar only when you choose to add a reminder. Calendar data for the current year is bundled in the app; other years are fetched once and then kept on your device for offline use.

May the lives of the saints and the rhythm of the Church year be a daily blessing.
```
